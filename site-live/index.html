Questions

Common questions

Do I have to pay anything every month?
No. It's a one-time fee and that's it. The only ongoing cost is web hosting, which you set up yourself — there are good options under $5/month, and we'll walk you through it so it's easy.
What if I don't have photos or a logo?
No problem at all. We can use professional stock photos that fit your trade. For a logo, a clean header with your business name looks great — plenty of our best builds have no logo at all.
How long does it take?
About 7 business days from the time we have your info (photos, services, contact details). Send your stuff quickly and it goes faster. Need it sooner? Just ask — rush delivery is available.
What does "you own it" actually mean?
When it's done, you get all the files plus every login (hosting, domain, and so on). You can take it to any other person down the road, move it anywhere, or change it yourself. We keep no control over it.
Will this get me to the top of Google?
We build every site so Google can clearly see who you are, what you do, and where you're located — and so it loads fast and works on phones. That's a strong start. Getting to the very top takes time and ongoing work, and we'll always be straight with you about what's included and what isn't.
Can I make changes myself after it's done?
Yes — that's what the training video is for. We'll show you how to update text and swap photos without touching any code. For bigger changes, our simple update pricing is always there.
