FAQ

Common questions

Do I need to pay anything monthly?
No. The $699 is a one-time fee and that's it. The only ongoing cost is web hosting, which you set up yourself — we recommend options under $5/month like Namecheap or SiteGround. We can walk you through it.
What if I don't have photos or a logo?
No problem at all. We can use professional stock photography that fits your trade. For a logo, we can use a clean text-based header with your business name. Many of our best-looking builds have no logo at all.
How long does it take?
About 7 business days from the time we have your content (photos, services list, contact info). You can speed this up by sending content quickly. Rush delivery is available — ask about pricing.
What does "you own it" actually mean?
At handover, you receive all exported files plus every login credential (hosting, domain, etc.). You can take it to any developer in the future, move it to any host, or make changes yourself. We have no ongoing access or control.
Will this help me rank on Google?
We build every site with solid SEO fundamentals — proper page titles, meta descriptions, heading structure, schema markup, fast load times, and mobile optimization. This is a strong foundation, though ranking competitively takes time and ongoing content. We're transparent about what $699 covers and what it doesn't.
Can I make changes myself after delivery?
Yes — that's the whole point of the training video. We'll show you how to update text, swap photos, and make basic edits without touching code. For anything bigger, our a la carte update pricing is always available.
